About

I haven't been film making for very long, I have in the past made the occasional Youtube video and vlog but only really got into film-making after buying a small compact camera, the Canon HS 700sx, in early 2015. I like using a small compact camera that I can take with me whereever I go and film whatever appears to me as I go about my day. I believe in the importance of what you shoot, not with what camera you shoot it. No matter how fancy your camera, if the content is boring then people won't watch it. I also have a ZOOM H2n microphone that I use for capturing ambience and high quality audio. I edit using Premiere Pro CC on the computers at university, or using free software if I am editing something short on my own (somewhat underpowered) laptop. I hope you enjoy the videos I have made, please get in contact if you have any questions!
