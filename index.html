Simon Rolph - Homepage

A quick introduction...

Hi, welcome to my internet space. I'm a final year undergraduate student at the University of Exeter, Penryn Campus studying BSc Conservation Biology and Ecology. I see the huge potential this era of technology can bring to conservation and I have developed a broad skills set including ecological modelling and data analysis in R, webdesign, video editing and filmmaking.

I created this website in January this year and it is an ongoing project to provide a platform to share my ideas and experience, and as a hands-on approach to learning HTML and webdesign. This website will always be evolving and improving so do check back or follow me on Twitter for updates!
